Steps to Make Pancakes

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  7. Serve hot with syrup or toppings of your choice.
